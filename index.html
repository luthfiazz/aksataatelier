About

COMPANY DESCRIPTION

CV Aksata Creative Design (Aksata) is a design and build contractor + furnituremanufacture company that was established in Bandung 2015.With the help of dedicated creative designers and qualified local craftsman, Aksata aims to enrich human quality life through its products and works. Our mission is to help you design and build your dream house, create or reinvent your space, and provide furniture to support your life activities using high quality materials at an affordable price and streamlined schedule.

AKSATA ATELIER

Aksata interior design + architecture division help you in designing and building your entire house, creating or redesigning your space from initial design sketches to final completion.

AKSATA GALLERY & WORKSHOP

Our furniture gallery & workshop offers ready stock and manufacture high quality custom built-in furniture which fit your personal taste, decorate your space, and support your activities.
